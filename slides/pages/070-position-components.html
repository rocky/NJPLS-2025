Position Information: Location, Location, Location...

Formally, we need a mapping from compiler-produced instructions to positions in the source text.

But what is a source-text position?

In everyday life, a location (which I will call a position) is a street address:


Jane Street Capital
250 Vesey Street
New York, New York

So with this background, let us dig in.

One thing a compiler needs to provide a debugger, or error crash routine is a mapping from a given running instruction or the instruction where an error occurred, to a place or position in the source text.
