My Story

Once upon a time in a land far far away ...

My first full-time computer job was at IBM T.J. Watson Research Center.

The project I was hired for was to help develop a compiler for IBM's RISC CPU called POWER.

Back then, it was called 801 which is the building number on the photo you see here. This building is about 40 miles North of here and a little bit East.

We needed a debugger for our compiler, and I was offered the opportunity write it. I declined.

I recall thinking, why would I want to do that? Instead, I would rather write a paper on using alpha-beta search to perform some specific code optimization.
