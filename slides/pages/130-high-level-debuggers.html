Debugger pattern for High-level Dynamic-Language Interpreters

What is a a High-Level Dynamic Language Interpreter?

  • Typically has an eval() or exec function
  • This allows it to support an interactive shell
  • Has introspection, e.g. looking at call stack
  • Often runs of of a high-level form (bytecode, tree-evaluator)
